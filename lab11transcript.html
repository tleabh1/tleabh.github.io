Hello everybody An album that I would like to talk about today is WORLDS by PORTER ROBINSON. Porter Robinson is a prominent American electronic producer from the Chapel Hill, North Carolina area. His album, Worlds, was released on August 12, 2014, as a way of an escape from reality. The tune that’s playing in the background is a song off from the Album called Sad Machine. Although it can seem a bit dark at times, this album has had a far deeper impact on me than any album I’ve ever listened to. What makes this album quite special is the live show experience. Porter Robinson toured Worlds from 2014-2018 selling out venues all over the world. I finally got the chance in 2016 to see the live show and I was completely blown away. I definitely would recommend Worlds to anyone who is interested in experimental, electronic music and is open to escaping from reality.
